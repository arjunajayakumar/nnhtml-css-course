Review of the day

7/6/2020

The takeout we got from Doma is probably the best we've had from any restaurant in the last few months. Generous portions, nicely packed, and absolutely no change in quality from when we could dine in. We got the omakase set and unagi don and both were incredible! I'm so happy that they're operating during these times and highly recommend ordering from them if you're looking for a treat yourself kind of meal.
